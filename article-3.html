StemWard

Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) exposes students to more complex mathematics and science concepts and aims to serve as a foundation for their future college degrees. The STEM strand encompasses many subject matters compared with the other SHS strands. Because of this, the students are represented with many concepts and ideas that they can apply to their daily life as well as in their future studies. STEM education gives an understanding of concepts and encourages knowledge application. To keep it short, its aim can be formulated in two simple actions: explore and experience. Given this includes numerous opportunities and skills development, students are free to exercise what they learn and embrace mistakes in a risk-free environment. Project-based learning and problem-solving help learners to form a special mindset. Its core is in flexibility and curiosity, which equips learners to respond to real-world challenges. This article contains the possible opportunity and skills development that will help you enhance the knowledge about STEM, it will give you the idea of the advantages of the STEM strand compared with the other tracks. This article will help you answer the doubts of choosing STEM and will blow your mind upon knowing the content.

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) track is a highly technical track that equips students with the skills necessary for pursuing further studies and professions in different fields along the lines of medicine, engineering, economics, and numerous job opportunities. With STEM careers growing, one of the advantages of the STEM strand in senior high school is that graduates can look forward to illustrious careers that directly contribute to building the economy and helping society. One of these is the opportunities that we might get from choosing this track such as having the scholarship; scholarship can help you achieve your education goals, and consequently help you pursue your career goals. Choosing the STEM track opens the door to many possibilities for successful and fruitful careers especially after pursuing further education. Secure highly respected professions in competitive industries by choosing STEM as your track. Equipped with three strands to choose from, the STEM track presents you with opportunities to specialize in. According to the Department of Education (DepEd) "Skills developed by students through STEM provide them with the foundation to succeed at school and beyond." With the help of the STEM Strand the qualifications of students for the opportunities awaits will be furthermore developed. From that point on, 75% of all the new jobs will require skills that's under the strand of STEM, 90% of jobs will need digital skills. To sum up everything that has been stated so far, STEM Strand does not only avail scholarship grants, but also the development of potential skills at student's logical reasoning, critical thinking, and problem solving. Therefore with the STEM students' capabilities, the probability of succeeding economic growth will highly adapt to what might happen in the future for the furthermore opportunities awaits. STEM education has a wealth of advantages over traditional education. It teaches digital literacy and helps students develop the right mindset that makes their capabilities illimitable.
